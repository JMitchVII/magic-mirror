NEW HORIZONS

Capitalism strikes again! Tom nook has once again roped countless people across the globe into a new hair brained scheme.

His newest plot exploits the most desparate of individuals: those seeking to live on the fringes of society. All the while bringing them into the fold of his ever expanding megacorporation

With the help of his brainwashed children he has lured a small band of three survivors into a deserted wasteland. Only Communism can save them now. Our heroes anxiously await the arrival of Isobel to make things right.
